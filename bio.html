Bio

Hi, I’m Walid Hasan. I am an incoming theoretical physics graduate student at the University of Manitoba, where I will be working with Professor Andrew Frey and Professor Margaret E. Carrington. I have completed my undergraduate degree in physics from Brac University in Bangladesh. Besides physics, I am deeply passionate about writing and journalism. Some of my writings have been featured in national newspapers in Bangladesh. During my undergrad, I was the news editor at our student-run campus newspaper, **BracU Express**. You can find a list of my published articles here. Currently I write on my substack called "Muktobihongo".
